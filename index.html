ABOUT THE EVENT

Celebrating Agricultural Biodiversity

Biodynamic agriculture, a holistic and regenerative farming approach, is gaining traction in Asia, offering significant potential for sustainable food production and environmental health. It emphasizes creating a self-sustaining farm ecosystem, minimizing reliance on external inputs, and fostering healthy soil and biodiversity.

To truly develop and transform biodynamic agriculture, we must incorporate diverse knowledge systems, including those rooted in the Himalayas.

Our Mission

Aiming to discover new perspectives on biodynamic agriculture in Asia, this conference will place special emphasis on holistic approaches and integrated studies.
